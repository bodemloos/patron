Floor plan that learns your room

Two queues, one source of truth, with a one-tap 86-list for when the saumon runs out at 21:30.

Course-aware ticketing

Why it matters. Drinks belong at the bar; mains belong in the kitchen. Patron routes every line automatically based on its category, so the chef never sees "1 × Duvel" mixed in with the entrées.
  • ✓Status flow per line. Pending → preparing → ready → served. Tap to advance; the floor plan updates in real time.
  • ✓86-list in one tap. Out of mussels? Toggle in the kitchen view; POS and the customer menu instantly grey them out.
  • ✓Modifier-aware. "Burger, no onion, side of fries instead of salad" arrives as a structured order line, not a freeform note.
  • ✓Quiet by design. No printer thermal-receipt clatter; tickets live on a tablet at the pass.

05

A menu that knows what it costs.

Two-tier categories, modifier-aware items, and recipes that decrement stock and feed your COGS automatically.

Items, categories, recipes

Why it matters. Your menu changes every season. Patron's two-tier category system maps onto a real menu (Drinks → Beers (tap) → Jour De Fête), and recipes turn every sale into a real-time cost number.
  • ✓Two-tier categories. Parent chips, sub-category chips. Same structure on POS, kitchen, and customer menu.
  • ✓Modifiers & sizes. Per-item small/medium/large with price deltas; modifier popup with structured options and freeform notes.
  • ✓Recipes feed COGS. Each item lists the stock it consumes; paying an order decrements stock and stamps the line with its real cost-of-goods.
  • ✓Per-category VAT. Drinks at 21 %, food at 12 % — categories override the global default automatically.

Live stock counts driven by real sales, low-stock alerts, and a printable shopping list grouped by supplier.

Stock, suppliers, shopping list

Why it matters. Stock-outs lose covers; over-ordering ties up cash. Patron knows exactly what you sold last week — and turns it into a one-click email order to each of your suppliers.
  • ✓Auto-decrement. Every paid order deducts each recipe's quantities. No manual count for what just walked out the door.
  • ✓Low-stock alerts. Set a minimum per item; under-stock items glow red across the app.
  • ✓Shopping list, by supplier. Grouped & suggested-quantity reorder list with a pre-filled mailto link per supplier.
  • ✓Cost per unit, kept honest. Update the supplier price; future COGS reflects it instantly.

Compliance built in, not bolted on.

Contracts, Dimona, DmfA, and FAVV/HACCP registratie — all the paperwork the inspector asks for, generated where the work happens.

Contracts · Dimona · RSZ · HACCP

Why it matters. Most apps make you stitch payroll, social-security, and food-safety together yourself. Patron treats them as native modules, with the right fields and the right wording in NL/FR.
  • ✓Belgian-statute contracts. Permanent, fixed-term, flexi-job, student, extra, interim, internship — with NL/FR clauses ready to sign in the app.
  • ✓Dimona & DmfA submission flow. Opening / closing declarations on hire and departure; quarterly hours batch from clocked shifts. Wired for the RSZ web service.
  • ✓FAVV temperature register. Per-equipment min/max bands; out-of-range readings prompt for corrective action and stay flagged in the audit log.
  • ✓Cleaning checklist with overdue alerts. Daily, weekly, monthly tasks with last-completion timestamps; overdue tiles glow red.
  • ✓Receiving log. Supplier, items, cold-chain temperature, packaging & expiry checks — with corrective action when something fails.
