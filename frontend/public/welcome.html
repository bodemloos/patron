What's inside

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Restaurant-grade features stitched together so the front of house, the kitchen, the bar, the back office, and your customers all read from the same picture.

Visual floor plan

Drag-and-drop tables across indoor and outdoor zones. Tap any table to open its order — colour-coded by status.

Slide-over POS

Tap an item to add it; configured sizes prompt for a pick. Mains and desserts open the modifier sheet for allergies and notes.

Reservations + Patronize

Manage bookings in-app and let visitors reserve via the embeddable widget. Per-weekday opening hours and exceptional closure periods (holidays, private events) flow straight to the customer-facing slot grid.

QR table-side ordering

Generate a QR per table. Guests scan, browse, and send pending lines straight to the kitchen — the waiter still pays out.

Premium KDS tickets

Push-based via SSE — no polling. Each table is a dark ticket card with a glowing urgency bar (fresh / aging / late), course-grouped lines, allergy pills, and per-line ✓ buttons.

Bar runs alongside

Drink lines route to a dedicated Bar view while the kitchen sees only food. Bartender role gets the same ticket UI plus an 86 list scoped to drink-category items.

Stock that closes the loop

Recipes auto-decrement on payment. A shopping-list view groups everything below threshold by supplier, with one-click email orders.

Staff, shifts & schedules

Clock-in / clock-out plus a published schedule that toggles between week and month views. Planned hours and forecasted payroll cost roll up at the top.

Belgian contracts & RSZ

Generate signed PDFs for vast, flexi, student, extra, interim, and stage contracts. Submit Dimona-in/out and quarterly DmfA hours batches, with a full audit trail of every declaration.

VAT, Z-reports, exports

Per-category tax rates, per-rate VAT breakdowns, end-of-day Z-reports, and CSV exports your accountant will actually like.

Customer loyalty

Reservations and paid orders quietly build a customer record. Mark VIPs, capture allergy notes, see lifetime spend.

Patronize widget

A reservation pill on every page of your website.

Drop one script tag into your HTML. A "Make a reservation" pill pins to the bottom-right; tapping it opens the full booking flow inside an iframe.

  • ✓ One <script> tag — no CSS, no setup, no JS API to call.
  • ✓ Bookings land in your Reservations list with a "via widget" tag.
  • ✓ Auto-assigns the smallest fitting table, prevents double-booking.
  • ✓ Honors prefers-color-scheme — looks native on light or dark sites.
  • ✓ Cross-origin friendly via data-api override.

Table-side ordering

A QR per table. The whole menu in their pocket.

Edit a table in the floor plan, click the QR button, print the SVG. Pin it to the table. That's it.

  • ✓ Generates PNG and SVG QR codes per table, downloadable and printable.
  • ✓ "Print all QR codes" lays out the whole restaurant on one printable sheet.
  • ✓ Customers see the same dark-themed menu, send lines straight to the kitchen.
  • ✓ Waiter still finalises payment — guests don't see the till.
Belgian-ready

Built for the Belgian horeca rulebook.

VAT, contracts, and social-security declarations baked in, so a manager isn't living half in this app and half in spreadsheets.

Official contracts, signed and printed

Pick a statute (vast, bepaalde duur, flexi-job, student, extra, interim, stage) and generate a print-ready Dutch contract with the right legal clauses, INSZ, IBAN, address, PC 302 references — straight to "Save as PDF" from the browser.

Dimona + DmfA submission

Submit Dimona-in / Dimona-out for any contract; aggregate clocked shift hours per worker for the quarterly DmfA. Every payload stored as an audit-trail record. Plug your own RSZ web-service credentials when you're ready to go live.

VAT split per category

Set 6% / 12% / 21% per category and Patron tags every line at create time. Z-report breaks the day's totals down by rate, ready for your accountant.

Closures + per-day hours

Set the regular weekly opening hours per day, plus exceptional closure periods for holidays and private events. The booking widget stops offering slots on those dates and tells the customer why.
